ABOUT US

Our History

  • In year 1962, PIChE Cebu Chapter was formed. Founded by Engr. Elsa P. Roska, Engr. Antonio “Dodong” V. Osmeña, Engr. Antonio A. Enriquez, Sr., and a group of Cebuano chemical engineers in fulfillment of the need for a local professional organization.

  • In year 1987, PIChE Cebu was duly established and registered at the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as a non-stock, non-profit organization by 15 Cebuano chemical engineers as its incorporators with the primary purpose of promoting and developing the Chemical Engineering profession through the active participation and involvement of its members.

  • Year X , Organized the 1st ever PIChE national convention, having gathered at least XXXX participants.

  • In 2021, PIChE Cebu ratified its new organization and bylaws. By doing so, we aim to ensure that our practices and policies are in line with the changing needs of our stakeholders and the society we serve
