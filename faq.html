To become an active member of PIChE – Cebu Chapter, the member must be able to pay their annual dues to the Chapter.

The total annual due for renewing members is 500 PHP. To renew your membership, visit bit.ly/PIChECebuRegister.

The membership fee for new members is 1600 PHP. To join PIChE – Cebu Chapter, visit bit.ly/PIChECebuRegister.

For a member to obtain a certificate of good standing, the member must be an active member of the chapter and has paid their annual dues to the chapter. The member must also state their purpose for acquiring a certificate of good standing. To start the process, send an email to king@cebu.piche.org.ph.

A Professional Dry Seal is a personal identification seal to certify official documents of the chemical engineering practice. SEALbisyong PIChE Cebu is an initiative of PIChE – Cebu Chapter to help members obtain a professional dry seal. Visit this link.
