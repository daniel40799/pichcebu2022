Atong Handumanan

A screenshot of our collective memories and legacies – PIChE Cebu launches its first Handumanan in the hope of virtually memorializing the shared history of our members and partners. This digital wall is composed of pixels that can be owned perpetually. Owners can put photos, messages, and even URL links for our viewers. Proceeds go to the social programs and initiatives of PIChE Cebu

Cebu Water Summit

With the support of Nature's Spring, the Philippine Institute of Chemical Engineers - Cebu Chapter joins forces with the University of San Carlos - Water Resources Center and the PUM Netherlands to gather all the key policy & decision makers from all sectors of Cebu to address the island's water crises.

Pakighimamat

Cebu Chemical Engineers’ Conference. Pakighimamat is the Cebuano for meeting up or making rapport to another. This 2022 PIChE Cebu is proud to reinvent its annual General Assembly to the first Pakighimamat - an avenue where reputable Chemical Engineers of Cebu have discourse on topics relevant to the profession and society, be informed of the developments of the organization, and elect a new set of Board Directors.
