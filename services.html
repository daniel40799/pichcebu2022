Our Services

As a professional organization of chemical engineers, we offer a broad range of services to advance the chemical engineering practice in the Philippines. You can find opportunities for continuous professional development here, from upskilling courses to refresher training.

Browse through our catalog to choose the right service for you.

Professional Development

CPD Seminars

Enhance your skills while stocking up on CPD points with our CPD-accredited seminars and conferences.

Corporate Trainings

We offer training on a variety of chemical engineering-related topics, as well as refreshers on permitting and compliance, for you and your company.

Virtual Webinars

Gain knowledge and education wherever you are in the Philippines (or the world) with our virtual webinars.

Professional Services

SEALbisyong PIChE Cebu

Obtain your personal identification seal to certify documents of the Chemical Engineering practice.

Tambayayong

An initiative to grow PIChE Cebu Chapter into the biggest network of Chemical Engineers through a membership drive.

Annual Events

Pagpahalipay: A Testimonial and Congratulatory Dinner

Pagpahalipay is an oath-taking event for newly registered and licensed Chemical Engineers in Cebu.

Pakighimamat: The PIChE Cebu General Assembly

Meet and establish rapport with the Chemical Engineers in Cebu through Pakighimamat, the PIChE Cebu General Assembly.
